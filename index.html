About

Right Directions

Right Directions started as a dream 13- years ago. The aim was to build an educational consultancy that specializes in the field of overseas education to deliver the world-class as well as reliable counseling to Indian students planning to study abroad. Focused vision and evolving mission help us expanded as a leading educational consultancy in North India with a corporate office set up in Yamunanagar, Haryana in 2006 with a network of elite branches across Haryana, Telangana, Gujarat, Chandigarh, and Nepal.

We learn and change every day based on the current requirements and expectations of the universities and the students that we represent. As we continue to set high standards of quality we are constantly growing our network of offices in India

International students in Canada

On February 12, 2021, Canada announced a further reduction of eligibility requirements for Post-Graduation Work Permits (PGWPs), as international students continue to be impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic. The new rules allow “international students to complete their entire program online from abroad and still be eligible for a PGWP.” These loosened requirements apply to “all international students who are enrolled in a PGWP-eligible program, and meet all other PGWP criteria.”
